In 2023, many companies shifted to remote work, allowing employees to collaborate across 3 different time zones without difficulty. The average online meeting now lasts around 27 minutes.

The human brain can process around 11 million bits of information per second, but only about 40 bits reach conscious awareness. This means more than 99% of what we experience is filtered automatically.

A study found that typing 30 minutes a day for 21 consecutive days can noticeably improve muscle memory and finger placement skills. Small consistent practice beats intense irregular work.

In a survey of 1,500 students, nearly 72% reported that learning with short sessions throughout the day was more effective than studying for long periods at once. Breaks help the brain retain information.

The first computer to store programs electronically was the Manchester Baby in 1948. More than 75 years later, modern smartphones have over 1,000,000 times more computing power.
